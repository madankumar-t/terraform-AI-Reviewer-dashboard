- **Total Reviews**: Cumulative count with trend indicator

- **Risk Distribution**: Percentage breakdown by risk level
- **Risk Trend**: Historical trend showing improvement/decline
- **Top Findings**: Most common security issues
- **Cost Opportunities**: Potential cost savings
